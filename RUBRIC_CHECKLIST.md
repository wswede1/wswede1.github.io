## Peer Review Questions

Ask a classmate, friend, or mentor to review your site and answer:

1. **First Impression**: What's your overall impression of the site? Does it look professional?

2. **Introduction**: After reading the introduction, do you have a clear sense of who I am and what I can do?

3. **Projects**: Are the project descriptions clear and engaging? Can you understand what I did and why it matters?

4. **Learning Outcomes**: Can you identify how I demonstrated each of the four learning outcomes?

5. **Code**: Do the code examples make sense? Are they well-explained?

6. **Issues**: Did you notice any typos, grammatical errors, or broken links?

7. **Hiring Manager Perspective**: If you were hiring for an NLP role, would this portfolio make you want to interview me?

8. **Suggestions**: What's one thing I could improve?
